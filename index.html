Our Story

Born from fandom.

Dhriti — the Sanskrit word for determination, discipline, and mental strength. We chose that name because being a real fan takes all of those things.

We started as fans who wanted merchandise that actually felt premium. Not cheap fast-fashion prints. Not flimsy keychains. Real, collectible, gift-worthy items made with care — at prices that don't make you wince.

Today we make merchandise for sports fans, anime lovers, gamers, and every other passionate community in between. The common thread? People who go beyond casual. People who go beyond talent.
